Reference network of a Autumn 2016 course

Limitations

About data

References to following references is not included:

  • Optional readings from the syllabus. Some of these items do get referenced by the students.
  • Academic publications from outside the syllabus
  • Online sources are not included (see prev.). These include a wide range of things, including Obama's statements on Whitehouse.gov, Facebook's policy documents, Wikipedia, news stories from BusinessInsider, Danish media, Google, Apple and Facebook statements, Zuckerberg's status posts, lecture slides etc.
  • Chapters of books are not separated, because referencing which the sudents reference is a bit of a drag. This impacts especially Connolly+Hoar 2013 and DeNardis 2014.

Further, the data was typed in from a 100 or so PDF documents, with inconsistent references, so human errors possibly exist from just the typing process.
